Brew Guide

What makes a great cup of chai?

Good chai is about layering ingredients in the right order so the tea tastes strong, the milk feels smooth and the spices remain clear instead of muddy.

1. Tea First

Boil water with Assam leaves to build body before adding milk.

2. Spice Release

Crush ginger or cardamom lightly so the oils infuse without turning bitter.

3. Simmer Slowly

A short simmer makes the texture creamy and lets the aroma settle into the tea.

When chai feels best

  • Morning: stronger brew with less sugar for a sharper start.
  • Afternoon: pair with a snack for the most balanced experience.
  • Evening: elaichi chai works well when you want aroma over intensity.
